## Running

### Short interval running
- 5-10 min warmup 
- 30 sec sprint 60 sec job (5-6 times)
- 5 min cooldown jog

### 4x4 interval jogs
- 1 min sprint 
- 3 min jog
- light jog 10 min cooldown

### Tabata Workout
- 20 seconds of high intensity jog
- 10 second walk periods x8

### Sprint Walk Jog Repeat
- 20 second sprint
- 1 min walk
- 2-3 min jog

Repeat for 45 min to simulate a half of soccer

- 1-2 min jog
- 8-10 rounds

## Running to be outstanding

1. Week 1: Run 4 days, 5 miles each day, at a normal place. You can sprinkle in 4-6 strides afterwards for a few of these days if you feel good. 20 miles total

2. Week 2: Run 4 days, but one day run 8 miles, all at normal paces. One of your runs not the (8 mile) integrate 6-8x30sec of comfortably hard running uphill. If you encounter any hill, press it for 30 seconds. Then return to a normal pace. Repeat till you do 6-8 of those. 23 miles total. 

3. Week 3: Run 5 days, 3 of them being 5 miles, one being a very easy 3 miles, and an 8 mile run. Again, pick one of the 5 mile runs to complete the intgerated hill running as described in the previous week 26 miles total.

4. Week 4: Run 4 days, 2 days running 5 miles, one running 7, and one 8. On the 7 mile run day, run 2 miles easy, then 3 miles at a comfortably hard pace and finish 2 miles easy, One of the 5 mile days should include the hills from before. 25 miles.
